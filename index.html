You are given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the randomly generated number on the screen.

The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked on.

The value of each crystal will change when you start an auction.
